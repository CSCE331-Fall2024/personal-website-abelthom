Flash Card App

At home, I’ve always spoken Malayalam, my native language, but whenever I tried to read or write it, I lacked in confidence. To improve my skills, I decided to develop a flashcard app. The app features the most common Malayalam phrases and words alongside their English translations, helping users learn the language’s script. This project became even more rewarding as it turned into a collaborative effort with my mom, who helped translate some of the more difficult Malayalam words. Now, I can proudly say I’m continually improving in my native tongue.
